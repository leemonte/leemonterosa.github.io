WHAT IS MULTIPLE SCLEROSIS?

Multiple sclerosis (MS) is an unpredictable disease of the central nervous system that disrupts the flow of information within the brain, and between the brain and body.

View Signs and Symptoms

WHAT CAUSES IT TO DEVELOP?

The exact cause of MS is unknown, but we do know that something triggers the immune system to attack the brain and spinal cord. The resulting damage to myelin, the protective layer insulating wire-like nerve fibers, disrupts signals to and from the brain.

TYPES OF MULTIPLE SCLEROSIS

There are four types of MS:Clinically isolated syndrome (CIS), Relapse-remitting MS (RRMS), Primary progressive MS (PPMS), Secondary progressive MS (SPMS).
